About Us

CSI, Child Scholar Initiative, is the educational out-reach arm of Pray, Mom Pray! At the CSI we work with indigent children in government-run schools in Africa by providing them access to enabling schooling experiences.

Our Vision:

To empower a child scholar by harnessing their potential through quality education.

What We Do

We reduce the population of out-of-school indigent pupils by providing them with basic educational materials at the beginning of every school session in collaboration with communities and administrators of government-run schools.
CSI identifies outstanding indigent scholars and provides full scholarship to graduating pupils towards their secondary education into a government secondary school.

Our Values

Excellence, Service, Intergrity, Equality.
